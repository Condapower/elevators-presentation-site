Symptom: Doors won’t close, “DLK” not made

  • Controller: Any swing/slider with door operator
  • Where it happens: Lobby floor
  1. Verify door zone sensor and vanes are aligned; clean target.
  2. Inspect interlock circuit continuity from car top to controller.
  3. Check operator limits: fully-close limit and nudging input.
  4. Reduce closing speed/torque for test; check obstructions/rollers.
  5. Simulate DLK with meter only (no jumpers for run); adjust interlock.

Tip: If DLK flickers, suspect broken cable at hinge/loop.
